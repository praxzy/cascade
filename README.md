# Cascade

Cascade is an hourly payment platform designed to pay employees on an hourly basis. It leverages the Solana blockchain to provide fast, transparent, and reliable payments for workers, ensuring that employees are compensated in real-time for the hours they work.

## Features

- **Hourly Payments:** Employees receive payments every hour, reducing waiting times and improving cash flow.
- **Solana Powered:** Built on the Solana blockchain for low fees and high throughput.
- **Transparency:** All transactions are recorded on-chain for auditability.
- **Easy Integration:** Simple onboarding for both employers and employees.
